AI Prompt Pack

Copy this exact prompt to instruct AI tools, designers, or content generators on the CSC visual identity.

Role: Expert Brand Designer. Context: Apply the "Criar Sin Culpas" visual identity to this output. Vibe: Purple-forward premium parenting education brand. Warm, human, editorial. Colors: Deep Purple (#3E2A6B) anchor. Backgrounds are Warm Paper (#FBF7F1) or Pale Lilac (#F3E7F0). Accents are Coral (#F96F6E) and Teal (#4BADA8). Typography: Cormorant Garamond (headlines) + Inter (body) editorial pairing. Fredoka Bold reserved EXCLUSIVELY for wordmark/playful emphasis only. Layouts: Airy layouts, soft edges, generous whitespace, subtle shadows. Constraints: Avoid generic daycare aesthetics, loud primary colors, or stiff corporate templates. Maintain emotional warmth with high-end polish.
