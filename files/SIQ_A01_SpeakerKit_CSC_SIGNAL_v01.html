About · Sobre Nari
ES

Nari Weaver es la creadora de Criar Sin Culpas, una plataforma de acompañamiento parental que ayuda a familias a enfrentar los desafíos de la crianza con claridad, conexión y sin culpa. Madre de dos hijos neurodivergentes, su metodología combina psicología infantil, comunicación no violenta y acompañamiento emocional en un sistema estructurado que los padres pueden seguir paso a paso.

EN

Nari Weaver is the creator of Criar Sin Culpas, a parenting support platform helping families navigate the challenges of modern parenting with clarity, connection, and without guilt. A mother of two neurodivergent children, her methodology combines child psychology, nonviolent communication, and emotional support into a structured system parents can follow step by step.

Core Talk · Conferencia Principal
El rol del mundo adulto en la crianza digital
Cómo acompañar a los hijos frente a riesgos como el grooming y la violencia online sin romper el vínculo familiar.
How to protect children from risks like grooming and online violence without breaking the family bond.
ES

Los niños de hoy crecen en un entorno digital que sus padres no experimentaron. Esta conferencia invita a padres y educadores a repensar su rol — no como guardianes que controlan, sino como adultos que acompañan. Nari presenta estrategias concretas para construir confianza, abrir conversaciones difíciles y establecer límites que protegen sin romper el vínculo. Porque la mejor protección no es el control: es la conexión.

EN

This talk invites parents and educators to rethink their role — not as gatekeepers who control, but as adults who accompany. Nari offers concrete strategies for building trust, opening difficult conversations, and establishing boundaries that protect without breaking the family connection. Because the best protection is not control — it is the relationship itself.

Available Talks · Conferencias Disponibles
Talk 01
El rol del mundo adulto en la crianza digital

How adults can guide children through digital risks like grooming and online violence without breaking the family bond. For parents, educators, and organizations working with children and adolescents.

Talk 02
Criar en la era digital sin culpa

Screens are not the enemy — they are part of our children's world. A clear framework for setting digital boundaries that respect development and protect the family relationship.

Talk 03
Conexión antes que control

Why control strategies fail and how the relationship is the most powerful tool in digital parenting. Grounded in attachment theory and nonviolent communication.

Audience Takeaways
  • How to guide children through digital risks without fear or rupture of the family bond
  • Strategies for discussing grooming and online violence clearly and without taboo
  • How to build digital trust at home through honest conversations
  • A framework for digital limits children respect because they understand the purpose
  • Tools for regulating screen use without conflict or parental guilt
Speaker Bio

Nari Weaver is a parenting educator, content creator, and founder of Criar Sin Culpas. For over a decade she has supported Spanish-speaking families navigating modern parenting with practical tools and without guilt. A mother of two neurodivergent children, her work integrates child psychology, nonviolent communication, and emotional support into an accessible system for parents of all backgrounds.
