Body copy / Captions

Layout Rhythm
  • Generous whitespace to let content breathe
  • Modular cards with soft 12px rounded corners
  • Calm hierarchy driven by typography size
  • Left-aligned copy for readability
  • Subtle editorial asymmetry
  • Soft, elegant drop shadows (no harsh outlines)
  • Thin separator rules using Soft Sand
  • No heavy, blocky corporate boxes

Image Direction

Portrait Direction

The founder portrait is identity-first.

  • Warm, candid, credible, and human
  • Natural light or softened studio light
  • Often uses a clean circular crop
  • Eye contact establishes trust and connection

Speaking & Workshop

The speaking image is proof-first.

  • Shows Nari in action, holding space
  • Rectangular, elegant crops (not circles)
  • Clean backgrounds, uncluttered environment
  • Softened conference light, no harsh flash
What to Avoid
Over-designed overlays Generic "stock-mom" energy Internal placeholder captions left in Heavy Instagram filters Corporate rigidity

Voice & Copy

How it should sound

The tone is warm, direct, editorial, useful, and trustworthy. Nari Fateha is a trusted voice on digital parenting, emotional regulation, limits, and connection. Keep copy concrete and human.

Translation Rules:
  • If deliverable is in Spanish, headers must be fully Spanish
  • Avoid mixed-language label systems
  • Never use internal notes like 'final' or 'print-ready'
